Welcome to the Central Virginia Visual Assessment Project!


This is a project to determine the most valued landscape elements in Central Virginia based on visual preferences. As a participant, you will be asked to evaluate a series of landscape photos taken in Central Virginia. This survey will take approximately 5 minutes.

Access the survey by submitting a quick personal profile on the next page. All information is confidential and will not be used outside of this project. By submitting your information, you agree to the above terms and will be taken to the survey.
